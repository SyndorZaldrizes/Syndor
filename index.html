The Maryland Student Legislature

For more than 35 years, the Maryland Student Legislature (MSL) has provided college students with an immersive experience in the workings of state government. MSL provides practical experience in drafting and debating bills, navigating parliamentary procedure, and understanding the legislative process. These hands-on experiences serve as a foundation for potential careers in government, law, and public service.

Our Chapter & Executive Board

The SMCM Chapter is the official delegation to the Maryland Student Legislature. We prepare students to research issues, write bills, and confidently participate in statewide sessions; whether it‚Äôs their first speech or their fiftieth.

What We Do on Campus

  • Host bill-writing and public speaking workshops.
  • Run mock committee hearings and floor debates.
  • Coordinate attendance at Fall & Spring statewide sessions.
  • Connect members with POSC-395 for academic credit.

Membership

All degree-seeking SMCM students are welcome. Active members are those who attend at least one out of every three meetings and help shape our legislative agenda.

Your Path Into Public Service

How a Semester in MSL Works

Whether you‚Äôre curious about law school, local government, or just want to get more comfortable speaking in front of a crowd, MSL gives you a real pathway to practice those skills in Maryland‚Äôs context.

1

Join the Chapter

Drop into a general meeting, get added to our roster, and meet the Executive Board and members.

2

Identify a Maryland issue that matters to you and use our templates to draft a bill or resolution.

3

4

Travel with the delegation to session, debate on the floor, and see your work move through the chamber.

Suggested First Steps

If you want the smooth onboarding path instead of raw chaos:

  • Pick an issue you actually care about (you’ll defend it out loud).
  • Decide: Act (bill) vs Resolution (statement/call-to-action).
  • Use the templates below and bring a draft to a workshop.
  • Practice a 60–90 second sponsor speech.

Before You Go to Session

Session Prep & Personal Toolkit

Beyond the text itself, preparation makes session way less stressful. Use these reminders as your baseline.

Personal Session Checklist

  • Bill/resolution draft saved + backed up
  • 1–2 minute sponsor speech outline ready
  • Committee assignment + chair confirmed
  • Travel/housing/dress code confirmed
  • Device charger + notebook + pen (yes, really)

For Committee & Floor Debate

  • Prep 2–3 likely questions + answers
  • Have a “friendly amendment” option if needed
  • Know your bill’s one-sentence purpose
  • Stay concise: clarity beats speeches

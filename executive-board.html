Executive Board – SMCM MSL Chapter

This page serves as a record of the students who have helped build and lead the SMCM Maryland Student Legislature Chapter. It’s a running archive of executive boards over time, beginning with our founding leadership team.

Founding Executive Board (2025–2026)

Our founding executive board helped bring MSL to campus, build the chapter’s structure, and represent SMCM in statewide sessions for the first time. Per Article VI, Amendment 2 was created to facilitate the creation of the MSL SMCM Chapter and does not represent the proposed blueprint for future governance of this chapter.

Delegation Chairperson

Nathan Sanchez Alvarado

Serves as the primary liaison to the Maryland Student Legislature, coordinates the delegation at sessions, and oversees chapter strategy.

Vice Delegation Chair / Attorney General

Aidan Smith & Jonathan Howell

Supports the Delegation Chair, tracks legislation coming from SMCM, and advises members on rules, procedures, and constitutionality.

President

Nathan S A

Leads on-campus operations, runs general meetings, and ensures members are prepared for statewide sessions and bill deadlines.

Treasurer

Matthew Jacobs & Ami Patel

Manages the chapter budget, coordinates with SGA and the SMCM Foundation, and tracks fundraising and Giving Tuesday efforts.

Secretary

Abigail Magnuson & Cheyenne Paslawskyj

Maintains meeting minutes, attendance records, and internal documentation so the chapter stays transparent and organized.

Communications Director

Taylor B. & Shannon Stapleton

Oversees social media, campus outreach, and promotional materials to keep SMCM students informed about MSL opportunities.

Ambassador / Outreach Officer

James McComas (25)

Builds connections with other campus organizations, departments, and delegations to help grow participation and collaborative projects.
